Ár Misean Our Mission

Iománaíocht agus camógaíocht a chothú trí Ghaeilge in Uíbh Ráthach — pobal oscailte, fáilteach a chruthú ina mbeidh deis ag gach duine óg i gCiarraí Theas ár gcluichí dúchais a imirt agus a gceangal lenár dteanga féin a neartú.

To grow hurling and camogie through the Irish language in Uíbh Ráthach — building an open, welcoming community where every young person in South Kerry has the chance to play our native games and strengthen their connection to our own language.

Is club nua iománaíochta agus camógaíochta lán-Ghaeilge é Uíbh Ráthach a bunaíodh le déanaí i gCiarraí Theas.

Tá sé tábhachtach dúinn go mbeadh an club oscailte do chách in Uíbh Ráthach agus táimid ag cur fáilte roimh dhaoine óga ón gceantar ar fad gur mhaith leo iománaíocht nó camógaíocht a imirt trí Ghaeilge.

Uíbh Ráthach is a new all-Irish hurling and camogie club that has recently been established in South Kerry.

It is important to us that the club is open to everyone in Uíbh Ráthach, and we welcome young people from all over the area who would like to play hurling or camogie through the medium of Irish.
